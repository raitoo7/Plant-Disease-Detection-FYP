About the System

Our Plant Disease Classification System assists farmers by providing a quick, reliable way to classify images of crops such as potato, strawberry, and corn into categories like healthy, diseased, or not a leaf using deep learning models.

Model Selection

We experimented with VGG-19 and ResNet-50 v2. While VGG-19 showed promising results, ResNet-50 v2 provided better accuracy, leading us to choose it for the final model.

Training & Impact

The model was trained using a publicly available dataset from Mendeley, enhanced by our own curated "not a leaf" dataset from various sources. This combination ensures accurate classification of plant diseases and the ability to distinguish non-leaf images, empowering farmers with a robust and reliable tool.
